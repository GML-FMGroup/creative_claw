# product_detail_page

## Scope

HTML product detail page for product education, comparison, and purchase or contact intent.

## Standard Sections

1. Product hero
2. Key benefits
3. Product proof or specifications
4. Scenario details
5. Comparison or objection handling
6. Trust and support
7. Purchase, trial, or contact CTA

## Required Inputs

- Product name or category
- Product image when available
- Main selling points
- Target buyer
- Primary conversion action

## Design System Hints

- Product image or product state should be visible in the first viewport.
- Keep benefit copy concrete and tied to buyer needs.
- Use comparison and specs only when they help decision making.
- Avoid hiding product details behind decorative visuals.

## Variation Axes

- Product-first versus story-first hero
- Technical versus lifestyle emphasis
- CTA strength
- Specification density
- Scenario imagery

## Review Fields

- Product positioning
- Selling points
- Product image usage
- Buyer concerns
- CTA wording

## QC Focus

- Product is not cropped or obscured.
- Selling points are complete and visible.
- CTA remains reachable on mobile.
- Product references are tracked in production state.
- HTML and asset paths remain portable.
