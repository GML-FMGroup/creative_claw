# landing_page

## Scope

HTML landing page for marketing, conversion, product education, or campaign launch.

## Standard Sections

1. Hero
2. Social proof
3. Problem and value proposition
4. Feature system
5. Workflow or how it works
6. Use cases
7. Pricing teaser or contact CTA
8. FAQ
9. Final CTA

## Required Inputs

- Goal or offer
- Audience
- Primary action
- Brand or product references when available
- Constraints for tone, layout, and device targets

## Design System Hints

- Make the product or offer visible in the first viewport.
- Keep CTA treatment consistent across hero and final CTA.
- Prefer clear contrast and restrained surfaces over decorative effects.
- Use responsive section spacing that keeps the next section hinted below the hero.

## Variation Axes

- Hero composition
- Visual density
- CTA tone
- Color mood
- Proof emphasis

## Review Fields

- Audience
- Primary action
- Section order
- Visual tone
- Required assets

## QC Focus

- First viewport communicates the offer clearly.
- CTA is visible on desktop and mobile.
- Text does not overflow on mobile.
- Brand assets are used consistently.
- HTML remains portable without local absolute paths.
